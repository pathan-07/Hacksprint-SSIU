Privacy Policy – VoiceKhata

Last updated: 2026-01-15

What we collect

  • Shop phone number (as an identifier)
  • Customer names and transaction amounts (udhaar/payment entries)
  • Voice messages/transcripts if you use voice input
  • Technical logs (timestamps, request metadata) for debugging

How we use data

  • To record and manage udhaar/payment ledger entries
  • To provide summaries (e.g., customer total outstanding)
  • To send reminders/notifications you trigger (e.g., overdue holds)

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data with service providers required to operate the app (e.g., Meta WhatsApp Cloud API, Google Gemini, Supabase/Postgres) only for delivering the service.

Data retention

Ledger data is retained until you request deletion. Debug logs are retained for a limited period.

Security

We use access controls and secure transport (HTTPS) where applicable. No system is 100% secure.

Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
